Treat your emails like instant messages!

Everybody has an email address, so why not use it for real-time communication?
There is no need for an account creation - simply log in to one of your email accounts and your messages of the last few days will be fetched automatically.
After that the messenger with all of your latest contacts and messages opens in an instant messaging-style and you will receive notifications for every new mail within seconds.
